Warm Dusk

Night mode for Sony Google TV

Warm Dusk adds a warm, dimmable filter across compatible Sony BRAVIA Google TV apps and the home screen. It can ramp automatically every evening and provides a TV-native quick panel.

The optional Accessibility route enables a held remote-key shortcut. If you skip it, the Warm Dusk Panel remains available from the home row with the same controls.

Privacy: no internet permission, analytics, ads, account, microphone, or location. Settings stay on the TV.

Compatibility varies by BRAVIA model, Android/Google TV version, and HDR mode. Exact tested models will appear in the compatibility matrix after closed-test evidence.
