Warm Dusk

Press kit

Warm Dusk adds a warm, dimmable filter and quick remote panel across a TV. The main app is under 1 MB and has no internet permission, analytics, ads, or account.

Verified facts

  • Google TV and Fire TV still lack an OS-level night mode.
  • Free core: whole-TV filter, local automatic evenings, and quick panel.
  • Optional Accessibility shortcut; equivalent home-row panel fallback.
  • Store package com.warmdusk.tv; app source remains private.

Language guardrails

Warm Dusk is a display-comfort utility. It makes no sleep or medical claims. Some TV brands offer their own picture modes; Warm Dusk addresses Google TV and Fire TV OS coverage and compatibility varies by device.
