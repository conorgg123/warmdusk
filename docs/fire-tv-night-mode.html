Warm Dusk

Night mode for Fire TV

Warm Dusk is designed to add a warm, dimmable whole-screen filter where Fire TV has no built-in blue-light night mode. The preview remains available for sideloading by power users; a Fire TV store submission is pending owner action and device evidence.

The home-row panel is the dependable control path. Remote shortcut support varies by Fire TV model, remote keys, and Accessibility availability.

Privacy: the main app has no internet permission, analytics, ads, account, microphone, or location.

Compatibility is not universal. Exact Fire TV results remain PENDING until the closed-test matrix includes a real device.
